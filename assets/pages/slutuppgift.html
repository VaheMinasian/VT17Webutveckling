A word obout Apple Pie

An apple pie is a fruit pie, in which the principal filling ingredient is apple. It is, on occasion, served with whipped cream or ice cream on top, or alongside cheddar cheese. The pastry is generally used top-and-bottom, making it a double-crust pie; the upper crust may be a circular or a pastry lattice woven of crosswise strips. Depending on the baker's preference, the bottom of the double-crust may be baked first.... Exceptions are deep-dish apple pie, with a top crust only, and open-face Tarte Tatin.... It is also said that apple pie making was brought to America by English settlers. (source Wikipedia)
